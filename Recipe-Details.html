Main Course: Qabili Pulao

Qabuli Pulao is a delicious Afghan rice dish made with basmati rice, meat, and a variety of spices. Follow these steps to prepare this traditional meal.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice: 2 cups
  • Lamb or Chicken: 1 lb
  • Carrots: 2 (julienned)
  • Onion:1 (chopped)
  • Raisins:1/2 cup
  • Salt:To taste
  • Black Pepper:To taste
  • Cumin:1 tsp
  • Vegetable Oil:3 tbsp
  • Water:4 cups

Instructions

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add the meat (lamb or chicken) to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Stir in the cumin, salt, and black pepper. Add 4 cups of water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until the meat is tender.
  5. Once the meat is cooked, remove it from the pot and set aside. Strain the broth and reserve it.
  6. In the same pot, add the soaked rice and enough reserved broth to cover the rice. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and cook for about 20 minutes.
  7. In a separate pan, saut√© the julienned carrots in a little oil until soft. Add the raisins and cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  8. Once the rice is cooked, fluff it with a fork and layer the saut√©ed carrots and raisins on top.
  9. Serve the rice with the cooked meat on the side or on top.

International Cuisine: Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour: 250 grams
  • Warm water: 150 milliliters
  • Yeast: 1 teaspoon
  • Sugar: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Pizza sauce (or tomato sauce): 50 grams
  • Mozzarella cheese: 200 grams
  • Fresh basil leaves: 5-6 leaves
  • Olive oil for drizzle: 1 tablespoon

Instructions

    Preparing the Dough:
  1. Dissolve the yeast with sugar in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes to activate.
  2. In a large bowl, add flour and salt, then make a well in the center.
  3. Pour the yeast mixture and olive oil into the flour and mix with your hands or a spoon to form a dough.
  4. Knead the dough on a flat surface until smooth and elastic (about 5 minutes).
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it, and let it rest in a warm place for 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
    6. Assembling the Pizza:
  6. After resting, divide the dough into two parts (for two pizzas).
  7. Roll out each piece of dough into a circle about 25 cm in diameter.
  8. Spread pizza sauce over each dough circle.
  9. Grate mozzarella cheese and sprinkle it on top of the sauce.
  10. Bake the pizza in a preheated oven at 220¬∞C (450¬∞F) for about 10-12 minutes, or until the dough is cooked and the cheese is melted.

Instructions:
